Ninja Kiwi, previously known as Kaiparasoft Ltd, is a mobile and online video game developer.

Ninja Kiwi’s games

Ninja Kiwi’s first game was a browser based game called Cash Sprint, developed on the Adobe Flash Platform.

Since then, they have produced more than 60 games across platforms including Adobe Flash, Android, iOS, PlayStation Portable, Nintendo DS, and more recently, Steam. Their most well-known titles are the Bloons and Bloons Tower Defense games. In 2012, Ninja Kiwi purchased Digital Goldfish, a Dundee, Scotland-based developer, for an undisclosed sum.

Is Ninja Kiwi shutting down?

as of January 12th 2021, the Flash Games on the Ninja Kiwi website will stop working. This was a decision taken by Adobe to end their Flash support and is outwith our control.

We did have a solution to this which was the Ninja Kiwi Archive on Steam (a free bundle of our website games which we planned to still work after Flash’s end of life), but after further testing, it looks like that may no longer be viable and now these games may be inaccessible (at least temporarily if not permanently) as of the date above. We are actively looking into alternative options to keep these games alive, so be sure to keep an eye on our website’s blog for updates on that, but we can’t make any promises at this stage.
